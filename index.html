Kepler's Birthday Cake

Serves 1-ish

Ingredients

  • chicken breast
  • rice
  • peanut butter

Directions

  • Make some rice. Cook it slow and low to make it a bit more malleable.
  • For the faint of heart, feel free to pause here and let the rice finish before moving on. You've earned it. Join the rest of us on the next step whenever you're ready.
  • Now, let's boil a chicken breast. Somewhere between 5 and 20 minutes. Feel it out.
  • Once the rice is done, form it into a cake-shape. I used a bowl and just squashed it all down in there.
  • Put some peanut butter in your favorite brand of resealable sandwich bag. Seal it up all nice, and put it in a bowl. Put that bowl over the pot of boiling water from your chicken and leave it there to get nice and fluid.
  • Take out your chicken, cut it up, arrange it on your rice-cake.
  • Cut a corner on your warm, melted peanut butter and drizzle it all over your masterpiece.
  • Enjoy.
